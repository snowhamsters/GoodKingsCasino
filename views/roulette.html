The Roulette Wheel:

The roulette wheel has numbered pockets ranging from 0 to 36.

The numbers are alternately colored red and black, except for the 0, which is green.

The arrangement of numbers on the wheel is the European version.

Players place their bets on the roulette table layout before the wheel is spun.

Inside Bets:

Straight Bet: Betting on a single number.

Outside Bets:

These are bets placed outside the number grid, typically betting on larger groups of numbers or characteristics of the numbers.

Red or Black: Betting on whether the ball will land on a red or black pocket.

Odd or Even: Betting on whether the ball will land on an odd or even number.

Low or High: Betting on whether the ball will land on a low (1-18) or high (19-36) number.

Dozen: Betting on one of three groups of 12 numbers (1-12, 13-24, or 25-36).

Column: Normally, betting on one of three vertical columns of numbers. On our table this is betting on the three horizontal rows of numbers.

Spinning the Wheel:

Once a bet is placed, the roulette wheel can spin.

the ball falls into one of the numbered pockets on the wheel.
